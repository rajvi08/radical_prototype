Conceptual Terminology:

Modulon – From Latin modul- (modular, unit) + -on; represents a modular, self-contained unit integrating multiple services.

Energetron – From Greek energeia (energy, activity, work) + -tron; a device/unit managing energy flows and storage.

Totalis – From Latin totalis = complete, whole; symbolizes a fully integrated, all-in-one system.

PolyStruct – From Greek poly- = many + struct (structure); a multi-service, structural unit.

UniBrix – From Latin uni- = one + brix (block); a single modular block integrating multiple services.
